========================
1. STUDENT REGISTRATION FORM
========================

Collect the following fields:

- Username
- Full Name
- Email (Gmail)
- Phone Number
- Father / Mother Name
- Age

IF Age < 18:

IF Age >= 18:

- Profile Picture (required, clear face)

- Last Education Level

2. STUDENT VERIFICATION FLOW
========================

After registration:

STEP 1:

STEP 2:

STEP 3:

STEP 4:
- After test:
